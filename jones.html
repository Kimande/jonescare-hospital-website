ABOUT US

Learn more about our commitment to your health and wellbeing

JONESCARE LTD is a fully registered Level 2 outpatient health facility located in Webuye Town, Bungoma County. We are dedicated to providing affordable, timely, and high-quality healthcare services to individuals and families in the community.

Our team of experienced healthcare professionals is driven by compassion and professionalism, ensuring each patient receives personalized care tailored to their specific needs.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a patient-centered approach, we strive to be your trusted healthcare partner for all your medical needs.
